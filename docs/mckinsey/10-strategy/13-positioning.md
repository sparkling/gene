## McKinsey Definition of Positioning

**Positioning** answers: *"What mental space do we own in the customer's mind relative to competitors?"*

| Layer | Question | Focus | Audience |
|-------|----------|-------|----------|
| Vision | What future are we building? | Aspiration | Everyone |
| Mission | What does the company do? | Direction | Employees, investors |
| Value Prop | Why should I choose you? | Purchase argument | Customers deciding |
| **Positioning** | **What mental space do we own?** | **Competitive perception** | **Internal teams, marketing** |

---

## McKinsey Positioning Format

> FOR [target] WHO [need/problem], [BRAND] IS THE [category] THAT [key benefit]. UNLIKE [competitors], WE [unique differentiator].

**Positioning must answer:**
1. Who is the target customer?
2. What category do we compete in (or create)?
3. What is our unique claim to that space?
4. How are we different from alternatives?

## THREE WORLDS Framework

The platform uniquely bridges THREE distinct knowledge domains:

```
                    THREE WORLDS
                         │
    ┌────────────────────┼────────────────────┐
    │                    │                    │
WORLD 1              WORLD 2              WORLD 3
MODERN               TRADITIONAL          NUTRITIONAL
GENETICS             MEDICINE             SCIENCE
    │                    │                    │
┌───┴───┐            ┌───┴───┐            ┌───┴───┐
│ dbSNP │            │ TCMSP │            │ USDA  │
│ClinVar│            │IMPPAT │            │ FooDB │
│PhrmGKB│            │KampoDB│            │OpenFF │
│SNPedia│            │ HERB  │            │Phenol │
└───────┘            └───────┘            └───────┘
    │                    │                    │
 1.2B+               100K+                380K+
 SNPs               compounds              foods
```

### World Definitions

| World | Description | Examples | Integration |
|-------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| **1. Modern Genetics** | The science of DNA, SNPs, pathways | dbSNP, ClinVar, PharmGKB, Reactome | Gene variants, drug interactions, pathway analysis |
| **2. Traditional Medicine** | Ancient healing wisdom from global traditions | TCM herbs, Ayurveda, Kampo, Western herbal | Herb-gene connections, pattern matching, treatment suggestions |
| **3. Nutritional Science** | The science of food, nutrients, and their effects | USDA FoodData Central, FooDB, nutrient databases | Nutrigenomics, food recommendations, cofactor needs |

### Why THREE Matters

- TWO worlds (genetics + traditional medicine) leaves out the practical daily intervention
- Food and nutrition is what people actually DO every day
- Nutritional science is the bridge between understanding (genetics) and action (eating)
- Traditional medicine already includes food as medicine; modern nutrition adds scientific rigor

## Segment Analysis

### Segment Options Considered

| Option | Segment | Pros | Cons |
|--------|---------|------|------|
| A | Everyone seeking health understanding | Largest TAM | No differentiation |
| B | Biohackers/optimizers | High willingness to pay | Saturated, competitive |
| **C** | **"The Unhelped" (chronic illness)** | **Underserved, motivated, strong WTP** | **Narrower initial market** |
| D | Practitioners (B2B) | Higher ARPU | Different product needed |

### Decision: Option C — Pick One Segment, Own It

**Primary Segment:** Those failed by conventional medicine ("The Unhelped")

**Characteristics:**
- Chronic illness patients (ME/CFS, autoimmune, Long COVID)
- Mystery illness investigators
- Undiagnosed or poorly managed conditions
- Spent years seeking answers
- Often dismissed by conventional doctors

### Why "The Unhelped" as Primary

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Pain intensity** | HIGH — desperately seeking solutions |
| **Willingness to pay** | HIGH — already spending on alternatives |
| **Word of mouth** | HIGH — active in communities, share discoveries |
| **Competition** | LOW — competitors focus on biohackers |
| **Mission alignment** | HIGH — founder's personal journey |

### Secondary Segments (Future Expansion)

| Segment | Timing | Positioning Adjustment |
|---------|--------|------------------------|
| Biohackers/optimizers | Phase 2+ | "Beyond optimization — understand your biology" |
| Parents investigating children | Phase 2+ | "For families seeking answers" |
| Preventive seekers | Phase 3+ | "Know your risks before symptoms" |
| Practitioners (B2B) | Phase 3+ | Separate positioning needed |
