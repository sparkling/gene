## Glossary

| Term | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| `biological process` | A recognized series of molecular events with a defined beginning and end | Cell division, apoptosis |
| `molecular function` | The biochemical activity of a gene product | Kinase activity, DNA binding |
| `cellular component` | A location within a cell where a gene product is active | Mitochondrion, nucleus |
| `enzyme classification` | Hierarchical numbering system for enzymes based on reaction catalyzed | EC 1.1.1.1 (alcohol dehydrogenase) |
| `reaction kinetics` | Quantitative parameters describing enzyme reaction rates | Km = 0.5 mM, Vmax = 100 umol/min |
| `ontology term` | A controlled vocabulary entry with defined relationships to other terms | GO:0006412 (translation) |

### Domain-Specific Terms

| Term | Definition | Related To |
|------|------------|------------|
| `Gene Ontology (GO)` | Standard ontology for gene product attributes across all organisms | Functional annotation |
| `Rhea` | Expert-curated database of biochemical reactions with ChEBI linkage | Reaction data |
| `BRENDA` | Comprehensive enzyme database with functional and kinetic data | Enzyme information |
| `IntEnz` | Integrated enzyme database with official EC nomenclature | EC classification |
| `SABIO-RK` | Database of biochemical reaction kinetics with experimental conditions | Kinetics data |
| `BioModels` | Repository for computational models of biological systems | Systems biology |
| `ChEBI` | Chemical Entities of Biological Interest - ontology for small molecules | Metabolite identifiers |
| `Human Protein Atlas` | Database mapping human proteins to cells, tissues, and organs | Protein expression |
