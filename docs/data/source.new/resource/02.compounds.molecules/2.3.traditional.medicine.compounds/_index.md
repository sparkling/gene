# Traditional Medicine Compounds

**Parent:** [Compounds & Molecules](../_index.md)

## Overview

This subcategory provides cross-references to compound databases specifically focused on traditional medicine systems. The primary traditional medicine databases are organized in Category 5 (Traditional Medicine).

For comprehensive traditional medicine compound data, see the databases organized under Traditional Chinese Medicine, Ayurveda, Kampo, and other traditional systems in Category 5.
