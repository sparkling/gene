---

## Summary

OMIM has a tiered licensing model. The web interface is free for personal, educational, and research use. Downloading bulk data requires registration. Commercial use and data redistribution require a paid license from Johns Hopkins University.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data (free use):

- Use omim.org for personal study
- Use for educational purposes
- Use for academic research
- Access via the web interface

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Download bulk data without registration
- Commercial redistribution of OMIM data
- Incorporate into commercial software
- Redisplay OMIM data in applications without license

---

## Special Terms

### Academic Download Access

Single-user academic access requires free registration with an institutional email.

### Commercial Licensing

Commercial use requires a license from Johns Hopkins Technology Ventures. Contact JHTV-OMIM@jhmi.edu.

### Penalty Clause

Unauthorized access may result in penalties of double the standard license fee.
