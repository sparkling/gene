## Summary

HPO is made available under an open access license that permits use and redistribution for any purpose including commercial applications. Users must cite the HPO appropriately and may not modify the ontology content or logical relationships. The ontology is copyrighted to ensure integrity.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Integrate into clinical decision support systems
- Build phenotype matching and diagnostic tools
- Redistribute with proper attribution
- Create applications using HPO terms

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without compliance:

- Modify the ontology content or logical relationships
- Edit term definitions or hierarchy
- Use without citation of HPO Consortium
- Remove copyright notices

---

## Special Terms

### Ontology Integrity

HPO is copyrighted to protect the integrity of the ontology. All modifications must be made through official HPO development channels.

### Publications License

HPO publications are under CC BY 4.0.
