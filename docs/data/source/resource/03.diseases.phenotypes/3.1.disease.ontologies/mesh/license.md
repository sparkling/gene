## Summary

MeSH is produced by the National Library of Medicine (NLM), a US federal government agency. As a US government work, MeSH is in the public domain and freely available for any use without restriction, including commercial applications.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Redistribute without restriction
- Create commercial medical indexing products
- Build literature search and text mining tools
- Integrate into healthcare systems

---

## Restrictions

There are no restrictions on use of this public domain data.
