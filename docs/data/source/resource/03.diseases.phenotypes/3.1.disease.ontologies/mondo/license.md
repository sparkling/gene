## Summary

MONDO is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0). This permissive license allows unrestricted use, including commercial applications, provided appropriate credit is given to the Monarch Initiative.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Integrate into clinical data systems
- Create derivative ontologies and databases
- Redistribute with attribution
- Use for rare disease patient matching

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without compliance:

- Use without proper attribution
- Claim endorsement by Monarch Initiative
