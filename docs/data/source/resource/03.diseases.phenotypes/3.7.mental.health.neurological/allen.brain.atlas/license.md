## Summary

Allen Brain Atlas data is available for research and non-commercial purposes without additional permission. Commercial use requires written permission from the Allen Institute. Proper citation is required for all uses including academic publications.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for research and non-commercial purposes
- Publish in scholarly journals with proper citation
- Create derivative works for research
- Access via API and web interface
- Download data for academic analysis

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial redistribution of Content
- Incorporate into commercial products for sale
- Use Content to perform commercial services
- Redistribute without attribution

---

## Special Terms

### Academic Publications

Publication of limited Content in scholarly, professional, or journalistic publications is permitted with appropriate citation.

### Commercial Licensing

Commercial use requires written permission from the Allen Institute. Contact them via their website for licensing arrangements.
