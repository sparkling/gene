# DisGeNET License Terms

License details for DisGeNET disease-gene association database data usage.

---

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| License | CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 |

| Attribution Required | Yes |
| Redistribution | Non-commercial, ShareAlike |

---

## Summary

DisGeNET is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International License for academic use. Commercial use requires a separate license agreement with DisGeNET.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data (academic):

- Use for academic and non-commercial research
- Integrate into non-commercial research tools
- Publish derived analyses with proper citation
- Redistribute for academic purposes under same license

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use or redistribution
- Incorporate into commercial drug discovery platforms
- Redistribute under different license terms

---

## Special Terms

### Commercial Licensing

Commercial use requires contacting DisGeNET for licensing arrangements at https://www.disgenet.org/

### ShareAlike Requirement

Academic derivative works must be distributed under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.

When attribution is required, use this format:

```
License: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.
```
