## Summary

BATMAN-TCM 2.0 is distributed under CC BY-NC 4.0, permitting non-commercial use with attribution. Commercial use requires contacting the publisher (Oxford University Press) for licensing. The database provides TCM ingredient-target predictions and known interactions.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic research
- Non-commercial redistribution with attribution
- Create derivative works for non-commercial purposes
- Publish academic analyses and papers
- Integrate into non-commercial research tools

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use of any kind
- Integration into commercial products
- Commercial redistribution
- Use in for-profit services

## Special Terms

### Commercial Licensing

For commercial re-use, contact Oxford University Press (journals.permissions@oup.com) or the database maintainers.

### Server Availability

The BATMAN-TCM server may experience connectivity issues. Implement retry logic and appropriate delays between API calls.
