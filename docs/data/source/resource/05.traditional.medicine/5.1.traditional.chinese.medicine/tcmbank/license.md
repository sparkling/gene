## Summary

TCMBank is freely available for academic research and drug discovery studies. Commercial use requires contacting the database maintainers. The database emphasizes compound structures, drug-likeness, and ADMET predictions for TCM compounds.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic drug discovery research
- Download compound structures (SDF format)
- Virtual screening for academic purposes
- Publish research with citation
- ADMET and drug-likeness analysis

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use or product development
- Commercial virtual screening services
- Integration into commercial platforms
- Commercial redistribution

---

## Special Terms

### Structure Data

Compound structures are provided in multiple formats (SDF, SMILES) for computational chemistry applications.

### Drug-Likeness Data

ADMET predictions and drug-likeness scores are computational predictions and should be validated experimentally.
