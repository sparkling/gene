---

## Summary

SymMap is freely available for academic use. Commercial applications require contacting the database maintainers. The database uniquely bridges TCM symptoms to modern phenotypes (HPO) and molecular mechanisms.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic research
- Explore symptom-phenotype mappings
- Download data for non-commercial analysis
- Publish research with proper citation
- Network visualization for research

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use or integration
- Commercial redistribution
- Use in for-profit products or services
- Commercial data mining

---

## Special Terms

### Symptom-Phenotype Mapping

The TCM symptom to HPO mappings are curated by domain experts. Users should acknowledge this unique integration in publications.

### Data Export

Bulk data can be downloaded in TSV format for academic research purposes.
