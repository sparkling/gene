## Summary

TM-MC is freely available for academic research comparing multiple traditional medicine systems. The database integrates Ayurveda, TCM, and other regional systems. Commercial use requires contacting the database maintainers.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic comparative research
- Cross-system analysis (Ayurveda vs TCM)
- Download data for non-commercial analysis
- Publish research with citation
- Explore convergent therapeutic uses

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use or product development
- Commercial redistribution
- Use in for-profit services
- Integration into commercial platforms

---

## Special Terms

### Multi-System Coverage

TM-MC uniquely covers multiple traditional medicine systems (Ayurveda, TCM, Unani, Siddha, Kampo). Cross-system analyses should acknowledge this breadth.

### Therapeutic Term Mapping

Traditional therapeutic terms may not map precisely across systems. Curation represents expert interpretation.
