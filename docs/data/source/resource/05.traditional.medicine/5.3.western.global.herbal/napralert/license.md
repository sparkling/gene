# NAPRALERT License Terms

License details for NAPRALERT (Natural Products Alert) data usage.

---

## Quick Reference

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| License | Subscription-Based |
| SPDX Identifier | Custom |
| Commercial Use | Yes (with commercial license) |
| Attribution Required | Yes |
| Redistribution | No |

---

## Summary

NAPRALERT is a subscription-based database requiring licensing for full access. Academic institutions can obtain institutional subscriptions. Commercial users require commercial licenses. Limited free searches are available for evaluation.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Limited free searches for evaluation
- Full access with institutional subscription (academic)
- Full access with commercial license (industry)
- Publish research using NAPRALERT data (with citation)
- Request custom literature searches

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Full access without subscription
- Redistribute database content
- Bulk download without license
- Share subscription access

---

## Special Terms

### Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Access Level | Target Users |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Free | Limited queries | Evaluation |
| Academic | Unlimited institutional | Universities, research institutes |
| Commercial | Full access + custom reports | Pharmaceutical, nutraceutical companies |

### Historical Coverage

NAPRALERT contains literature from 1975 to present, representing 50+ years of systematic curation by pharmacognosy experts.

## Attribution Template

When attribution is required, use this format:

```
Natural product literature data from NAPRALERT, University of Illinois at Chicago (https://napralert.pharmacy.uic.edu/).
```

### Citation

```
Graham JG, et al. NAPRALERT database. J Nat Prod. (Use current citation from website)
```
