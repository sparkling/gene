## Summary

HIT 2.0 is freely available for academic research on experimentally validated herbal compound-target interactions. The database serves as a validation resource for prediction-based TCM databases. Commercial use requires contacting the database maintainers.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic research and validation
- Access experimentally confirmed interactions
- Download binding affinity data
- Publish research with citation
- Cross-reference with prediction databases

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use or drug development
- Commercial redistribution
- Use in for-profit services
- Integration into commercial platforms

---

## Special Terms

### Experimental Validation Focus

HIT 2.0 contains only experimentally validated interactions, making it the gold standard for validating computational predictions from other databases.

### Multi-System Coverage

The database integrates validated targets from TCM, Ayurveda, and Western herbal traditions.

### Binding Affinity Data

IC50, Ki, Kd, and EC50 values are included when available from literature.
