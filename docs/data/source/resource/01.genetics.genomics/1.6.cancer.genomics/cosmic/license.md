## Summary

COSMIC is free for academic and non-commercial research use with registration. Commercial use requires a separate commercial license from the Wellcome Sanger Institute. COSMIC is the world's largest resource for somatic mutations in cancer and includes the Cancer Gene Census and mutational signatures.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic research (with free registration)
- Use for non-commercial clinical research
- Integrate into academic publications
- Download mutation, CNV, fusion, and signature data

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use without commercial license
- Bulk redistribution
- Integration into commercial products without license
- Access without registration

---

## Special Terms

### License Tiers

| User Type | Access | Requirements |
|-----------|--------|--------------|
| Academic | Full download access | Free registration |
| Non-profit | Full download access | Free registration |
| Commercial (small) | Full access | Contact for pricing |
| Commercial (enterprise) | Full access + redistribution | Contact for pricing |

### Registration Process

1. Create account at cancer.sanger.ac.uk
2. Provide institutional details
3. Accept terms of use
4. Access granted immediately for academic users

### Data Components

| Component | Included | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Somatic mutations | Yes | Core database |
| Cancer Gene Census | Yes | Driver genes |
| Mutational signatures | Yes | SBS, DBS, ID |
| CNV data | Yes | Copy number |
| Fusions | Yes | Gene fusions |
| Drug resistance | Yes | Resistance mutations |

## Attribution Template

When using COSMIC data, use this format:

```
COSMIC data from the Wellcome Sanger Institute (https://cancer.sanger.ac.uk/cosmic).
```

### Citation

```
Tate JG, et al. COSMIC: the Catalogue Of Somatic Mutations In Cancer.
Nucleic Acids Res. 2019;47(D1):D941-D947.
```
