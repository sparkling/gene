## Summary

CIViC data is released under Creative Commons Zero (CC0 1.0), effectively placing it in the public domain. This is one of the most permissive licenses available, allowing any use without restriction including commercial applications. While attribution is not legally required, it is encouraged to acknowledge the community effort.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial
- Integrate into commercial products without restriction
- Redistribute in any form
- Modify and create derivative works
- Use without attribution (though citation encouraged)

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- None - data is public domain

---

## Special Terms

### Community-Driven

CIViC is a community-curated resource. Users are encouraged to contribute interpretations and evidence to improve the knowledgebase.

### VICC Harmonization

CIViC participates in the Variant Interpretation for Cancer Consortium (VICC) harmonization effort with OncoKB, CGI, and other knowledgebases.

### Evidence Quality

Evidence items have different levels and ratings. Users should consider evidence quality for clinical applications.

## Attribution Template

While not required, when citing CIViC, use this format:

```
CIViC data from https://civicdb.org/.
Licensed under CC0 1.0 (Public Domain).
```
