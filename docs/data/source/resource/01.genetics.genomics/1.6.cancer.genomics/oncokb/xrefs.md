## Integration Notes

OncoKB is a precision oncology knowledge base curated by Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center.

**Primary use (Cancer Genomics):** Oncogenic variant annotations, levels of evidence, therapeutic implications, FDA-recognized biomarkers.

**Secondary use (Cancer Oncology):** Treatment recommendations, clinical trial matching, resistance mechanisms, companion diagnostics.

OncoKB provides clinically actionable interpretations of cancer alterations, recognized by FDA for tumor profiling test validation and clinical decision support.
