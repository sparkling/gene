## Summary

The Cancer Gene Census is part of COSMIC and follows COSMIC license terms. It is free for academic and non-commercial research use with registration. Commercial use requires a separate commercial license from the Wellcome Sanger Institute. The CGC provides the authoritative list of genes causally implicated in cancer.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic research (with free registration)
- Use for non-commercial clinical research
- Integrate into academic publications
- Reference CGC gene classifications

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial redistribution without license
- Access without COSMIC registration
- Use in commercial products without commercial license
- Bulk redistribution of CGC data

---

## Special Terms

### License Tiers

| User Type | Access | Cost |
|-----------|--------|------|
| Academic/Non-profit | Full download access | Free |
| Non-commercial | Full download access | Free |
| Commercial | Full access + redistribution | Contact Wellcome Sanger |

### Registration Requirement

All data access requires free COSMIC registration. Provide institutional email and accept terms of use.

### Part of COSMIC

CGC is a component of the COSMIC database. Access to CGC data is through COSMIC download portal.

## Attribution Template

When using Cancer Gene Census data, use this format:

```
Cancer Gene Census data from COSMIC (https://cancer.sanger.ac.uk/census).
```
