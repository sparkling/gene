## Summary

dbVar is a public domain resource maintained by NCBI (National Center for Biotechnology Information). As a U.S. government work, dbVar data is not subject to copyright protection and is freely available for any use without restriction. Attribution is recommended but not legally required.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic and commercial research without restriction
- Integrate into commercial products and services
- Redistribute in original or modified form
- Publish derived analyses without permission
- Create derivative databases for structural variants

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- None - data is public domain

---

## Special Terms

### Data Exchange

dbVar synchronizes with the European Variation Archive (EVA/DGVa) through monthly data exchanges. Users should be aware that some submitted study data may have additional terms from original submitters.

When attribution is provided (recommended), use this format:

Public Domain.
