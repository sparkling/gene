## Summary

AlphaMissense predictions are released by Google DeepMind under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license. This permits commercial use, modification, and redistribution provided appropriate attribution is given. Note that the model weights are not released; only pre-computed predictions are available.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic research
- Use for commercial applications with attribution
- Integrate into diagnostic and clinical tools
- Redistribute with attribution
- Create derivative works

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Claim predictions as your own work without attribution
- Use the "DeepMind" or "AlphaMissense" trademark inappropriately
- Access model weights (not publicly released)

---

## Special Terms

### Model Weights

The AlphaMissense model weights are not publicly released. Only pre-computed predictions for all possible human missense variants are available under CC BY 4.0.

### Google Cloud Terms

Data hosted on Google Cloud Storage may be subject to Google Cloud Platform terms of service for access.
