# PubMed Central License Terms

License details for PubMed Central data usage.

---

## Quick Reference

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| License | Mixed (varies by article) |
| SPDX Identifier | Various (CC-BY, CC-BY-NC, etc.) |
| Commercial Use | Depends on article license |
| Attribution Required | Yes (for most articles) |
| Redistribution | Depends on article license |

---

## Summary

PubMed Central hosts articles with various licenses. The Open Access Subset contains articles with liberal reuse rights (CC-BY, CC-BY-NC). Each article specifies its license in the XML metadata. Always check individual article licenses before reuse.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with Open Access articles:

- Access full-text XML and PDF
- Use for text mining and research
- Redistribute with proper attribution
- Create derivative works (license dependent)

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Use CC-BY-NC articles commercially
- Remove attribution from any article
- Claim that NLM endorses your use
- Redistribute non-OA articles

---

## Special Terms

### Open Access Subset

The OA subset (~5 million articles) allows bulk download and text mining:
- FTP: ftp://ftp.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pub/pmc/oa_bulk/
- Check `oa_file_list.csv` for individual article licenses

### Author Manuscripts

NIH-funded author manuscripts may have different terms than publisher versions.

---

## Attribution Template

When attribution is required, use this format:

```
Article from PubMed Central (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/).
License: [Article-specific license].
```

### Citation

```
National Library of Medicine. PubMed Central [Internet]. Bethesda (MD): National Center for
Biotechnology Information; [cited YYYY Mon DD]. Available from: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/
```
