# PubMed

**Category:** [Literature](../../../_index.md) > [Scientific Literature](../_index.md)

## Overview

PubMed is the premier biomedical literature database, maintained by the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI) at the National Library of Medicine (NLM). It comprises more than 36 million citations for biomedical literature from MEDLINE, life science journals, and online books.

PubMed provides free access to abstracts and links to full-text content. Records are indexed with MeSH (Medical Subject Headings) terms enabling precise subject searching. PubMed integrates with other NCBI databases through Entrez.

PubMed is the essential starting point for biomedical literature searches and is universally used by researchers, clinicians, and students.
