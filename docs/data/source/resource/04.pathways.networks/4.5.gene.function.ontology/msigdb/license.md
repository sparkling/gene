# MSigDB License Terms

License details for MSigDB (Molecular Signatures Database) data usage.

---

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|

| Redistribution | Conditional |

---

## Summary

MSigDB (v6.0+) is primarily available under CC BY 4.0, permitting commercial use with attribution. However, certain gene sets derived from external sources (particularly KEGG) have additional restrictions that must be observed. Users must check specific gene set provenance for commercial applications.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use MSigDB gene sets for academic research
- Use Hallmark and most curated gene sets commercially
- Download GMT files for enrichment analysis
- Integrate into academic tools and pipelines
- Create derivative analyses with attribution

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use of KEGG-derived gene sets (CP:KEGG prefix)
- Commercial use of KEGG_MEDICUS gene sets
- Ignore source-specific restrictions
- Redistribute restricted gene sets commercially

---

## Special Terms

### KEGG Gene Sets

KEGG pathway-derived gene sets (CP:KEGG_ prefix) are copyrighted by Kanehisa Laboratories. Commercial use requires separate KEGG licensing.

### KEGG MEDICUS

KEGG MEDICUS gene sets are under CC BY-SA 4.0, requiring ShareAlike for derivative works.

### GSEA Software

GSEA desktop software is under a BSD-style license, permitting redistribution with conditions.
