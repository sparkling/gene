## Summary

Reactome is fully open-access under CC BY 4.0, permitting unrestricted use including commercial applications. Users must provide appropriate attribution when using Reactome data. This makes Reactome an excellent open alternative to licensed pathway databases.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic and commercial research
- Integrate into commercial software and services
- Redistribute data with proper attribution
- Create derivative works and adaptations
- Publish analyses and visualizations

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Claim Reactome data as your own creation
- Remove or obscure attribution notices
- Use Reactome trademarks without permission

## Special Terms

### Software License

Reactome software tools are licensed under Apache 2.0, a permissive open-source license compatible with commercial use.

### Database Updates

Reactome releases quarterly updates. Users are encouraged to use the most recent version for accuracy.
