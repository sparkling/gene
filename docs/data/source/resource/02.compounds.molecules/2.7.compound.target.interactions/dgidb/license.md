## Summary

DGIdb is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0). This permissive license allows unrestricted use, including commercial applications, provided appropriate credit is given.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Integrate into drug target identification pipelines
- Create derivative databases and analyses
- Redistribute with attribution
- Build clinical decision support tools

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without compliance:

- Use without proper attribution
- Claim endorsement by DGIdb maintainers
