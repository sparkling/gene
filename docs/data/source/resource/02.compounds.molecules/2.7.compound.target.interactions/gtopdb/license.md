## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Integrate into pharmacology databases and tools
- Create derivative databases and analyses
- Redistribute with proper attribution

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without compliance:

- Redistribute under incompatible licenses
- Use without proper attribution
- Remove IUPHAR/BPS credit

## Special Terms

### ShareAlike Requirement

Derivative works must be distributed under CC BY-SA 4.0 or a compatible license.

### IUPHAR Official Status

GtoPdb is the official IUPHAR database, providing authoritative pharmacological nomenclature.
