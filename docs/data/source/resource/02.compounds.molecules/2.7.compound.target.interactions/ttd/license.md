## Summary

TTD is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 International License. The database is freely accessible for academic and non-commercial research. Commercial use requires contacting Oxford University Press for licensing arrangements.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic and non-commercial research
- Access the web interface without restriction
- Publish derived analyses with proper citation
- Download bulk data for research purposes

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial redistribution of the data
- Incorporate into commercial drug discovery platforms
- Use for commercial target validation without license

---

## Special Terms

### Commercial Licensing

For commercial re-use, contact Oxford University Press at journals.permissions@oup.com for licensing information.

TTD is freely accessible without login at https://idrblab.net/ttd/
