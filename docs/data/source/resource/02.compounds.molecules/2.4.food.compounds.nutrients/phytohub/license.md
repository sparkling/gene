## Summary

PhytoHub is described as a freely accessible electronic database for dietary phytochemicals and their metabolites. The database is freely available for academic research purposes. For commercial use or bulk data redistribution, users should contact the database maintainers at INRAE (formerly INRA).

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic and non-commercial research
- Access the web interface for metabolomics research
- Use for biomarker discovery research
- Cite in academic publications

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial redistribution of bulk data
- Incorporate into commercial products without permission
- Mass downloading without authorization

---

## Special Terms

### Academic Use

PhytoHub welcomes contributions from the research community and operates as an open community database for curation of phytochemical data.

### Commercial Inquiries

For commercial use or licensing, contact INRAE (Institut national de recherche pour l'agriculture, l'alimentation et l'environnement).
