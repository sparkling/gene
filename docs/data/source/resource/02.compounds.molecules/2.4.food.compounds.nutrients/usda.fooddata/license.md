---

## Summary

USDA FoodData Central is produced by the US Department of Agriculture, Agricultural Research Service. As a US government work, it is in the public domain and freely available for any use, including commercial applications, without restriction or licensing fees.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Redistribute without restriction
- Create commercial nutrition software and apps
- Integrate into food labeling compliance systems
- Build dietary assessment tools

---

## Restrictions

There are no restrictions on use of this public domain data. However:

- Data quality varies by data type (Foundation vs. Branded Foods)
- Users should verify analytical methods for research applications
- Nutrient values are subject to updates
