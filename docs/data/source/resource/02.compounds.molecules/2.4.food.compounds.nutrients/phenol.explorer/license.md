## Summary

Phenol-Explorer is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 3.0 License. The database is freely available for non-commercial academic research. Commercial use requires explicit permission from the authors and acknowledgment of the source material.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic and non-commercial research
- Integrate into non-commercial nutrition tools
- Publish derived analyses with proper citation
- Redistribute for non-commercial purposes

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial redistribution of the data
- Incorporate into commercial products
- Use for commercial dietary supplement development
- Commercial food product formulation without license

---

## Special Terms

### Commercial Licensing

For commercial use, contact the database authors for explicit permission. Commercial use requires:
- Written permission from the authors
- Explicit acknowledgment of Phenol-Explorer as the source
- Citation of original publications
