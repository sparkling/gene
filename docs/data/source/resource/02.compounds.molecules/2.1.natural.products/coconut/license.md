## Summary

COCONUT is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0). The database is openly available for all uses including commercial applications, requiring only proper attribution to the original source.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Integrate into commercial products and drug discovery pipelines
- Redistribute and create derivative datasets
- Modify and build upon the data

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Use without providing appropriate attribution
- Remove or obscure license notices
