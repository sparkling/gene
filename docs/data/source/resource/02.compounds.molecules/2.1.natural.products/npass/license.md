## Summary

NPASS is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 International License. The database is freely accessible for academic and non-commercial research purposes. Commercial use requires explicit permission from Oxford University Press.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic and non-commercial research
- Integrate into non-commercial tools and analyses
- Publish derived analyses with proper citation
- Redistribute for non-commercial purposes with attribution

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial redistribution or sale of the data
- Incorporate into commercial products or services
- Use for commercial drug discovery without license

---

## Special Terms

### Commercial Licensing

For commercial re-use of NPASS data, contact Oxford University Press at journals.permissions@oup.com to obtain appropriate licensing.
