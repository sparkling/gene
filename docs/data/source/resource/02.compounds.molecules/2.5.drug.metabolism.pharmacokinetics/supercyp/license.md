## Summary

SuperCYP is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0 License. The database is freely available for academic and non-commercial research. Commercial use requires contacting the maintainers at Charite Berlin for licensing arrangements.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic and non-commercial research
- Integrate into non-commercial drug interaction tools
- Publish derived analyses with proper citation
- Redistribute for non-commercial purposes under same terms

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial use or redistribution
- Incorporate into commercial drug development tools
- Redistribute under different license terms

---

## Special Terms

### ShareAlike Requirement

Derivative works must be distributed under CC BY-NC-SA 3.0 or compatible license.

### Commercial Inquiries

For commercial use, contact Charite - Universitatsmedizin Berlin for licensing information.

```
SuperCYP data from Charite Berlin (http://bioinformatics.charite.de/supercyp).

```

### Citation

```
Preissner S, et al. (2010) "SuperCYP: a comprehensive database on
Cytochrome P450 enzymes including a tool for analysis of CYP-drug interactions."
Nucleic Acids Res. 38(Database issue):D237-D243.
```
