## Summary

SwissADME is a free web tool provided by the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics. Licensed materials from SwissADME are available under CC BY 4.0. The tool is free to use for internal research (commercial and non-commercial). Redistribution of the tool itself or its content requires SIB written consent.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use the tool free of charge for any internal research
- Use for commercial internal research purposes
- Publish results with proper citation
- Build upon predictions for any purpose including commercial

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Copy, reproduce, or mirror the web tool
- Redistribute the tool or its underlying code
- Remove SIB attribution from the tool
- Create public mirrors without consent

---

## Special Terms

### Internal Research Use

"Permitted Use" includes private study and internal research, whether commercial or non-commercial.

### Citation Requirement

Results from SwissADME must cite the reference paper.

---

## Attribution Template

When citing SwissADME results, use this format:

```
ADME predictions from SwissADME (http://www.swissadme.ch/).
License: CC BY 4.0.
```

### Citation

```
Daina A, Michielin O, Zoete V. (2017) "SwissADME: a free web tool to evaluate
pharmacokinetics, drug-likeness and medicinal chemistry friendliness of small molecules."
Sci Rep. 7:42717. DOI: 10.1038/srep42717
```
