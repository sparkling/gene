## Summary

DailyMed is provided by the National Library of Medicine (NLM), a US federal government agency. As a US government work, the data is in the public domain and freely available for any use, including commercial applications, without restriction.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Redistribute without restriction
- Create derivative products and databases
- Integrate into clinical decision support systems
- Use for regulatory compliance applications

---

## Restrictions

There are no restrictions on use of this public domain data. However, note that:

- Drug label content reflects FDA-approved labeling
- SPL data should be cited appropriately in publications
- Users should verify currency of labeling information
