## Summary

ChEMBL is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License (CC BY-SA 3.0). This license permits commercial use, modification, and redistribution, provided that attribution is given and derivative works are shared under the same license terms.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Integrate into commercial drug discovery platforms
- Create derivative databases and tools
- Redistribute with proper attribution

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without compliance:

- Redistribute without attribution
- Create derivative works under incompatible licenses
- Remove or obscure license notices

---

## Special Terms

### ShareAlike Requirement

Derivative works must be distributed under CC BY-SA 3.0 or a compatible license.

### Data Integration

ChEMBL data may be combined with other databases, but the combined work should maintain attribution.

## Attribution Template

When attribution is required, use this format:

```
ChEMBL data from EMBL-EBI (https://www.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/).
License: CC BY-SA 3.0.
```
