License details for PubChem chemical information repository data usage.

## Summary

PubChem is produced by the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI), a division of the National Library of Medicine (NLM) at NIH. As a US government work, it is in the public domain and freely available for any use without restriction.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for any purpose including commercial applications
- Redistribute without restriction
- Create derivative databases and products
- Integrate into commercial chemistry software
- Build drug discovery pipelines

---

## Restrictions

There are no restrictions on use of this public domain data. However:

- Depositor-provided data may have individual terms
- Chemical structures should be validated for research use
- Bioassay data quality varies by source
