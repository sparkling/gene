---

## Summary

HOMD data is freely available for academic research and educational purposes. Commercial use requires permission. Citation required when publishing.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do:

- Use for academic research
- Download taxon and sequence data
- Use HOMT identifiers in publications
- Create derivative analyses (academic)

---

## Restrictions

- Commercial use requires permission
- Attribution to HOMD required
- Cite original publications
