## Summary

MetaHIT project data is publicly available for research use. The project has concluded but data remains accessible through archives. Attribution to MetaHIT Consortium required.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do:

- Use for academic research
- Download gene catalog data
- Perform meta-analyses
- Publish derived results with citation

---

## Restrictions

- Large commercial applications should contact authors
- Attribution to MetaHIT Consortium required
- Cite original Nature publications
