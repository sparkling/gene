## Summary

FooDB data is available under Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 International License. Academic and non-commercial use is freely permitted with proper attribution. Commercial applications require a separate licensing agreement with the FooDB team.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Use for academic research and publications
- Integrate into non-commercial educational tools
- Create derivative analyses for research purposes
- Share and redistribute with attribution (non-commercially)
- Modify and build upon the work for non-commercial purposes

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do without additional permission:

- Commercial redistribution of raw data
- Use in commercial products or services
- Sell access to FooDB data
- Remove attribution notices

---

## Special Terms

### Commercial Licensing

For commercial use of FooDB data, contact the FooDB team at the University of Alberta. Commercial licenses are negotiated on a case-by-case basis.
