## Summary

ConsumerLab data is proprietary and requires a paid subscription for access. Individual access costs $49.95/year. Commercial or enterprise use requires a separate licensing agreement. Data export and redistribution are not permitted without explicit authorization.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Access and view test results with subscription
- Reference findings in research with citation
- Use information for personal purchasing decisions
- Quote limited excerpts with attribution

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do:

- Redistribute raw data or test results
- Export data systematically
- Use commercially without license
- Scrape or bulk download
- Remove attribution notices
