## Summary

Natural Medicines (formerly Natural Medicines Comprehensive Database) is a proprietary clinical decision support resource. All content requires paid subscription. Data export and bulk download are not permitted. Enterprise licenses available for EHR integration.

---

## Permissions

What you CAN do with this data:

- Access with valid subscription (individual or institutional)
- Use for clinical decision making
- Generate patient handouts (subscriber feature)
- Reference with citation in publications
- EHR integration with enterprise license

---

## Restrictions

What you CANNOT do:

- Redistribute content in any form
- Export or bulk download data
- Use for commercial purposes without license
- Screen scrape or automated access
- Share login credentials
