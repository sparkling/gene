## Strategic Summary

### The Opportunity

**Platform:** Personalized health intelligence connecting genetics, traditional medicine, and nutrition.

**Vision:** Building a world where everyone can take charge of their physical and mental health.

**Mission:** We unite your biology with every medical tradition, so you can heal, thrive, and find the help and community you have been looking for.

**Tagline:** Your biology. Our wisdom. Community.

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 3 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Registered Users | 10,000 | 200,000 |
| Paying Users | 2,000 | 50,000 |
| MRR | $100K | $1M |
| ARR | $1.2M | $12M |

### THREE WORLDS Framework

| World | Description | Data Sources |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| Modern Genetics | SNPs, genes, pathways | dbSNP, ClinVar, PharmGKB (1.2B+ SNPs) |

## Framework Reference

### Governing Principles

**The Pyramid Principle (Barbara Minto)**
- Start with the answer/recommendation
- Group supporting arguments logically
- Ensure MECE coverage (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive)

**The Strategic Choices Cascade**

| Question | Maps To |
|----------|---------|
| What is our winning aspiration? | Vision, Mission |
| Where will we play? | Market, Segments, Geography |
| How will we win? | Value Proposition, Positioning |
| What capabilities must we have? | Product, Technology, Data |
| What management systems are required? | Operations, Processes |

### Document Template

All documents follow this structure:
1. **Header** — Document ID, Status, Owner, Date, Version
2. **TL;DR** — Maximum 3 sentences, answer first
3. **Key Decisions** — Decision table with rationale
4. **Analysis** — MECE breakdown with headers and tables
5. **Dependencies** — Links to related documents
6. **Open Questions** — Unresolved items
7. **Change Log** — Version history
